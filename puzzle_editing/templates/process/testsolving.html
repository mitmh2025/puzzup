Participating in a Testsolving Session

  1. (Optional) React to the weekly availability post to be pinged when the availability thread for that day is up.
  2. Give your availability for the day in the thread. A testsolving admin will start you on a session, often in a group.
  3. Your testsolving admin will give you specific instructions, but generally at this point, solve the puzzle as you would during a hunt. You will have a thread, voice chat, and sheet with your co-solvers.
  4. If you stop and plan to reconvene, provide intermediate feedback!
  5. If you are no longer having fun, you can stop, but please let your testsolving admin know and provide feedback.
  6. Once you have finished the session (hopefully by solving the puzzle), leave your final set of feedback.

Bella has written a guide on providing effective feedback.
